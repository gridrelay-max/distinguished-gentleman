Not Just a Haircut. A Tradition.

Since opening on Nolensville Pike, Distinguished Gentleman Barbershop has been the heartbeat of Nashville's southside community. Three generations of barbers. Thousands of haircuts. One unwavering standard: you leave looking sharper than when you walked in.

We still use the same hot lather machines, the same badger-hair brushes, and the same straight razors that legends of the trade sharpened on a leather strop. Some things don't need updating.
